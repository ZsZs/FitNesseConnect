!2 Verify Excel content - Fixture documentation and tests
!4 Purpose
This fixture facilitates to verify the content of an Excel sheet. It has many variants:
 * [[table matching verification][^TableMatchingVerification]] - compares the content of the provided !-FitNesse-! table with the subject Excel sheet
 * [[cell matching verification][^CellMatchingVerification]] - compares cell specifications with the cells in the subject Excel sheet
