!2 Verify Excel content - Fixture documentation and tests
!4 Purpose
This fixture facilitates to verify the content of an Excel sheet. It has many variants:
 * [[compare table verification][^CompareTableVerification]] - compares the content of the provided !-FitNesse-! table with the subject Excel sheet
 * [[cell specification verification][^CellSpecificationVerification]] - compares cell specifications with the cell contents in the subject Excel sheet
